Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a medical provider?

No. Many clients are entrepreneurs partnered with licensed providers. I help you think through structures that respect CPOM and state law; your attorney finalizes everything.

Can you guarantee a 60-day launch?

The framework is designed around ~60 days. Actual timing depends on your responsiveness, state requirements, and third-party approvals, but you’ll have clear milestones from day one.

Which states do you support?

I work with clients across the U.S. We factor in your state’s rules around supervision, telehealth, and ownership to decide if we’re a good fit.

Are medications or ad spend included?

No. You contract directly with pharmacies and the marketing agency. I help you select and structure those relationships so you’re not guessing.
