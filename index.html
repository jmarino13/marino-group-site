FAQs

Do I need to be a medical provider?

No. Many clients are entrepreneurs partnering with licensed providers. We help you think through structures that respect CPOM and state law, then your attorney finalizes everything.

Can you guarantee a 60-day launch?

We provide a 60-day launch framework and manage the build, but timing depends on your responsiveness, state requirements, and third-party approvals. You’ll have a clear timeline and milestones from day one.

Which states do you support?

We work with clinics across the U.S. and factor in your state’s rules around supervision, telehealth, and ownership. On the initial call, we’ll confirm fit and any special considerations.

Are medications and marketing spend included?

No. You contract directly with pharmacies and the marketing agency. We negotiate structure, access, and strategy so you can plug into proven partners instead of starting from scratch.
