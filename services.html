Frequently Asked Questions

These are the questions serious clients ask before they decide to move forward.

Do I need to be a medical provider to do this?

No. Many clients are entrepreneurs who partner with licensed providers. We help you think through compliant structures and introduce you to medical director and NP pathways so your attorney and providers can finalize the right model.

How realistic is the 60-day timeline?

The 60-day launch plan is aggressive but achievable for responsive clients. Your timeline depends on your state, decision speed, and third-party approvals. You will always have a clear roadmap and milestones.

What work do I have to do personally?

You focus on decisions — vision, budget, and hiring — while we handle structure, systems, and execution. We’ll tell you exactly what’s needed from you at each step so you’re never guessing.

Are medications and marketing spend included in your fee?

No. You contract directly with pharmacies and the marketing agency. Our role is to structure the relationships, strategy, and systems so your spend actually translates into patient volume and profit.

Can you help us add peptides, IVs, or hormones to an existing clinic?

Yes. We regularly work with med spas, dental practices, and existing clinics that are layering in GLP-1s, peptides, IV drips, and hormone optimization as new profit centers.

What’s the first step if I’m interested?

Book a strategy call. We’ll review your market, goals, budget, and timing — and if it’s a fit, we’ll outline exactly what working together looks like so you can make a confident decision.
