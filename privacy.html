Privacy Policy

We may use basic analytics tools that rely on cookies or similar technologies to understand how visitors use our site. This helps us improve content and performance.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with:

  • As required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. HIPAA and Protected Health Information

This website is intended for business and consulting discussions, not for sharing protected health information (PHI) about individual patients. Do not submit PHI through our website forms. Any HIPAA obligations between you and your own patients are your responsibility.

8. Your Choices

10. Contact
